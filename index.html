About Les Plats

Les Plats aims to maintain its tradition and continue to pass its exquisite, tasty, addicting pastry recipes. It was founded by Andre Salazar on February 28, 1892 amidst the war of France. Les Plats served as a supplement for the people — it saved them from hunger. As the business continues to grow, Les Plats' legacy is still spread by people.
